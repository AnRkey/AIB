Getting Started with AI Browser

AI Browser allows you to access multiple AI services in a single application with a tabbed interface.

Basic Navigation

Opening AI Services

Click on any of the AI service buttons in the toolbar to open that service in a new tab:

  1. Click ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Grok button in the toolbar.
  2. The service will open in a new tab.
  3. You can have multiple services open simultaneously in different tabs.

Working with Tabs

Manage your open tabs to multitask across different AI services:

  1. Click on a tab to switch to it.
  2. Click the Ã— icon on a tab to close it.
  3. Use the scroll buttons (< and >) to navigate when you have many tabs open.

Keyboard Shortcuts

AI Browser supports several keyboard shortcuts for efficient navigation:

Function Shortcut
New Tab Ctrl + T
Close Tab Ctrl + W
Refresh Ctrl + R or F5
Next Tab Ctrl + Tab
Previous Tab Ctrl + Shift + Tab
Open DevTools Ctrl + Shift + I

Advanced Features

Always on Top

Keep the AI Browser window visible even when using other applications:

  1. Click the AOT button in the toolbar.
  2. The button will highlight when Always on Top is active.
  3. Click again to disable Always on Top mode.

Opening a New Window

Create a new instance of AI Browser:

  1. Click the +Window button in the toolbar.
  2. A new AI Browser window will open.
  3. Each window operates independently with its own tabs and settings.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

If you encounter problems with AI Browser:

  • Service not loading: Click the refresh button or try reopening the tab.
  • Login issues: Some services may require you to log in again after periods of inactivity.
  • Performance problems: Close unused tabs to free up memory.

If problems persist, visit the Support page for more assistance.
