Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I log in to some AI services?
Some AI services may require additional authentication. Make sure you're logged in to the service's account and check if there are any browser compatibility issues.
How do I save my conversation history?
Conversation history is managed by each individual AI service. Use the export or save functionality provided by the service you're using.
Can I use keyboard shortcuts?
Yes, AI Browser supports standard browser shortcuts like Ctrl+T for new tab, Ctrl+W to close a tab, and Ctrl+R to refresh.
How do I clear my browsing data?
Go to Settings > Privacy and click on "Clear browsing data". You can choose what types of data to clear.
